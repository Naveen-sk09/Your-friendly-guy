Spiderman is an extremely popular comic character among the children. The children consider Spiderman to be their hero and inspiration too. It is a fictional superhero which is created by writer-editor Stan Lee and writer-artist Steve Ditko. The Spiderman character appears in American comic books, and Marvel Comics publishes it. The character is portrayed as an orphan named Peter Parker, who is being raised by his Aunt and Uncle Ben in the New York City.
His parents Richard and Mary Parker died in an air crash. Initially, the character of Peter Parker was depicted to be pale and inactive, who used to arrive late everywhere. But later on getting the power of Spiderman, he became very active, smart, fearless and responsible.
